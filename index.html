Skills

A reference for the 4 base skills for bartending.

Shaking This is the fundamental technique of bartending. Holding the shaker sideways about shoulder height; pulsate it back and forth for 10 seconds.
Stirring Using a bar spoon, slowly stir the cocktail around the rim for 15 seconds.
Throwing A bit of coordination is needed. With two shakers, pour the cocktail from one shaker to another 3 to 5 times.
Blending With a blender combine cocktail with ice and blend on low for about 30 seconds. Used mostly for exotic cocktails.
